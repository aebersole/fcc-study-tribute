Kurt Donald Cobain

The man who changed rock and roll

From birth to Nirvana

  • February 20th, 1967 - Kurt Cobain is born in Aberdeen, Washington to parents Wendy and Donald Cobain.
  • April 24th, 1970 - Kurt's little sister Kimberly was born.
  • 1976 - Kurt's parents get divorced. He would later go on record saying their divorce had a profound effect on him.

"I remember feeling ashamed, for some reason. I was ashamed of my parents. I couldn't face some of my friends at school anymore, because I desperately wanted to have the classic, you know, typical family. Mother, Father. I wanted that security, so I resented my parents for quite a few years because of that."

-Kurt Cobain
  • February 20th, 1981 - Kurt Cobain's 14th birthday. His uncle gave him a choice between a bike and a guitar. Kurt chose the guitar.
  • 1985 - Kurt formed the band Fecal Matter after he dropped out of highschool. During this time he met Krist Novoselic while hanging out at he Melvins practices. They would practice together above Krist's mothers salon.
  • 1987 - After trying for months to convince him, Kurt Cobain and Krist Novoselic formed the beginings of Nirvana.

From Nirvana to the Hall of Fame

  • June 15th, 1989 - Nirvana's first album, Bleach is released. This record was released with Chad Channing as drummer.
  • September 24th, 1991 - Nirvana's famous album, Nevermind, releases. Nirvana skyrockets to international fame. The song, Smells Like Teen Spirit, is a huge success and changes the face of alternative music forever.

“To me, Smells Like Teen Spirit reminds me a little bit of how Bob Dylan’s songs affected people in the 60s. In a way, I feel the song affected a generation of kids in the 90s. They could relate to it.”

-Butch Vig, Producer on Nevermind
  • February 24th, 1992 - Kurt was married to Courtney Love on Waikiki Beach in Hawaii.
  • August 18th, 1992 - Kurt and Courtney welcome their daughter, Francis Bean Cobain, into the world.
  • September 21st, 1993 - The album, In Utero, releases continuning Nirvana's hold on the billboard charts.
  • November 18th, 1993 - Nirvana performs at MTV's Unplugged at Sony Music Studios in New York City. This will later be released as an album that is regarded as one of Nirvana's best.
  • April 5th, 1994 - Kurt Cobain died from a self inflicted gunshot wound. His body would be found by friends on April 8th at his home in Seattle, Washington.
  • 2014 - Nirvana was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.

"Words suck. I mean, everything has been said. Words aren’t as important as the energy derived from music, especially live."

-Kurt Cobain
